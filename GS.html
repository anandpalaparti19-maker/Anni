A Letter Written in Starlight Dearest Swapna,

They arranged our meeting, but I chose you — every single day since. From that first moment when I saw you across the room, something in me went completely, irreversibly still.

You were arranged by family, but you were destined by the universe. Every morning you make chai before I ask. Every night you laugh at my terrible jokes. And in every ordinary moment, you are extraordinary.

They said arranged marriages are built, not fallen into. But I think I fell into you like a river finds the sea — inevitably, joyfully, without return.

Thank you for saying yes. For every year since. For being Swapna — mine, ours, always.

With all my love, Govardhan
